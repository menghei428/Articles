# Published Research Articles
I publish research on AI infrastructure, semiconductor economics, and regulatory dynamics in Leeds Finsights, a student-led financial analysis platform.

### Featured — Investment & Technology Analysis

**[Elon Musk's $25 Billion Bid to Rewrite the Chip Industry](https://www.leedsfinsights.com/post/elon-musk-s-25-billion-bid-to-rewrite-the-chip-industry)**

**Focus:** Semiconductor vertical integration, capital intensity, strategic control of AI compute
Analyses Terafab's semiconductor strategy as a capital allocation bet, with implications for TSMC dependency, AI compute pricing, and US industrial policy.


**[Can Anthropic Tell the Pentagon No?](https://www.leedsfinsights.com/post/can-anthropic-tell-the-pentagon-no)**

**Focus:** AI governance, defence contracts, strategic autonomy
Examines regulatory and commercial risk at the intersection of AI governance and defence procurement — with relevance to AI sector investment.

**[Why the UK's AI Boom Is Hitting an Energy Wall](https://www.leedsfinsights.com/post/why-the-uk-s-ai-boom-is-hitting-an-energy-wall)**

**Focus:** AI infrastructure economics, energy constraints, data centre ROI
Data-driven critique of grid infrastructure bottlenecks and their impact on UK tech investment attractiveness vs. Nordic and US markets.

**[Greenwashing and Market Failure: The Blind Spot in ESG Regulation](https://www.leedsfinsights.com/post/greenwashing-and-market-failure-the-blind-spot-in-esg-regulation)**

**Focus:** Information asymmetry, ESG signalling distortion, regulatory gaps
Applies information asymmetry theory to ESG capital markets, arguing that Scope 3 opacity enables systematic accounting arbitrage and distorts sustainable investment signals.
